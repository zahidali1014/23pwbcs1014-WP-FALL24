Cardio Workout

Boost your heart health and endurance with this intense cardio workout designed to burn calories and improve stamina. Perfect for all fitness levels.

Push-Ups

Push-ups are a staple in fitness routines because they effectively target multiple muscle groups. When you perform a push-up, you primarily work your chest (pectorals), shoulders (deltoids), and triceps. Beyond these, push-ups also engage your core muscles, including the abdominals and lower back, to maintain stability

Yoga

Yoga is a mind-body practice that combines physical postures, breathing exercises, and meditation. It enhances flexibility, strength, and relaxation. Common poses include Downward Dog, Child's Pose, and Warrior. Just a few minutes a day can improve both physical and mental health
