Computer Science

Computer science is the study of computers, computational systems, and the theory behind their operations. It encompasses a wide range of topics, including algorithms, data structures, software development, artificial intelligence, and cybersecurity. At its core, computer science focuses on problem-solving, using programming languages to write instructions that enable computers to perform specific tasks. With the rapid advancement of technology, computer science plays a crucial role in shaping innovations like machine learning, cloud computing, and mobile applications, making it an essential field in today’s digital world. It drives industries ranging from healthcare and finance to entertainment and education, revolutionizing how we live and work
